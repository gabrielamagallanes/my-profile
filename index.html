My Story

Where I'm From

I was born and raised in Washington D.C., the capital of the US. It's a lovely city! Coming from Mexican parents, I also get to spend my summers in Mexico. Growing up bilingual and having two cultures has been such an enriching and exciting experience!

How'd I end up in France?

Believe it or not, it all started with a giraffe! Back when I was 13 years old, on a bright Sunday morning, I stumbled upon an ad in the newspaper of an image of a giraffe with the caption: "Want to Study Abroad?". Back then, I had no idea what studying abroad was, but I still wanted to go for it! So, being only 13 years old, I left home & ended up in France for a year. Left fascinated with the country & the language, I decided to come back again for college. I love it here!

What I love

I'm a girl with many passions! I love languages, traveling, socializing, kickboxing, weight lifting, hiking, skiing, cooking & volunteering!
